The Tortoise and the Hare

  1. The race is won when one of the racers moves beyond 100 spaces.
  2. The tortoise to the right will race your choice of rabbit, below. Just click to pick.
  3. The racer will move the number of spaces shown in "speed" each turn the racer is focused.
  4. The racer will start with the number of focus shown, and will lose 1 focus per turn.
  5. When the racer's focus is < 1, the racer will stop racing until refocused.
  6. Rabbits will refocus when the turtle catches up. Turtles refocus after just 1 turn.
  7. More agility means less chance the racer will be caught and eaten.
